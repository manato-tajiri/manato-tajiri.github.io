Peer-reviewed, accepted (採録決定). To appear in *Journal of Natural Language Processing* Vol. 33, No. 2, on 15 June 2026.

Acceptance announced by the Association for Natural Language Processing (Japan) — see the [official 採択論文 page](https://www.anlp.jp/guide/saitaku.html) (search "田尻 愛斗").

This work extends our CRS × DPO framework to handle long dialogue contexts, improving recommendation response generation when the dialogue history is too long for a short window.
